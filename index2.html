Learn About E-Waste

Harmful Components of E-Waste

E-waste contains harmful substances such as lead, mercury, cadmium, and brominated flame retardants. Improper disposal can lead to contamination of soil, water, and air.

Effects on Environment and Health

Improper disposal of e-waste can contribute to pollution, soil degradation, and water contamination. Exposure to hazardous substances can have adverse effects on human health, including respiratory and neurological disorders.
